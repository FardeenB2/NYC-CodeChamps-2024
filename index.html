Test Your Brains!

Measure Your Techno-ness

Step into the arena of coding innovation, merge creative genius, make dreams a reality! Release your skills, fuel your enthusiasm, and together let's create the future! Let's get hacking!

Code Champs 2024 is dedicated to fostering innovation and creativity through coding for middle school and high school students throughout New York. Our commitment to providing transformative learning extends beyond competition. Through Code Champs we hope to ignite a passion for young pioneers and future tech leaders.

We believe that every young mind is a universe of untapped potential. Our hackathon is designed to be a launchpad for the next generation of innovators, creators, and problem-solvers. We aim to provide a platform where middle and high school students can explore their passions, collaborate with like-minded peers, and bring their imaginative ideas to life.

Hackathons simulate real-world challenges and provide students with invaluable hands-on experience. They will learn how to work collaboratively in teams, manage their time effectively, and overcome obstacles. These skills are essential for success in both academic and professional pursuits. We want to equip students with the tools they need to thrive in the future.

x

Our ultimate goal is to inspire the next generation of leaders and change-makers. By participating in our hackathon, students will develop a growth mindset, a passion for learning, and a desire to make a positive impact on the world. We hope to ignite their curiosity, fuel their creativity, and empower them to become the innovators of tomorrow.

FAQs

How do I register?
Click on the register link above, and fill out the Google Form provided.
How long is the Hackathon?
12 hours, The Hackathon would run from 7am-7pm.
How many people can be in a team?
A team must be composed of 2-4 people.
Who is eligible to join?
Middle School and High School students.
What will be the theme for the Hackathon?
Theme will be announced during the opening ceremony.
What do I need to bring?
You can bring your own laptop.
Will food be provided?
Yes, Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner will be provided.
Can we use Scratch and Roblox?
Yes you can utilize Scratch and Roblox.
